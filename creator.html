How it Works

Glad you asked. Buy Me a Drink links to your social media channels, creating a donation hub for your fans and followers all in one place. Fans simply donate a drink, coffee or anything they like! You'll then recieve the (retail) value of that gift. You can also set gift options or amounts for the different content you produce. We never rescrict your ability to recieve gifts from content you've made.

1 Sign Up

Create an account with us to link your social accounts and content. Your personalised link will appear on all of your content descriptions for fans to donate to your Buy Me a Drink account.

2 Create

Carry on creating the amazing content that your fans love so much, with the added benefit that at the click of a button they can donate a gift to you!

3 Get Paid

You'll then be gifted amounts deposited in to the bank account linked to your Buy Me A Drink account. With our simple Creator portal you can easily view and manage the content your fans can apprceiate the most.
