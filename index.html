Buy Me a Drink is the new easy way to say thank you to friends, family, podcasters, your boyfriends neighbours sister in law… basically anyone.

How it Works

Glad you asked. Buy Me a Drink links to all social media platforms, so you can send the value of a drink, coffee or even a burger to someone you know or really admire. Did that barista slide you a free crossaint yesterday? Say thanks with a pint. Want to keep supporting your favourite YouTube make up artist? Buy them a new make-up brush?

01 Sign Up

through Facebook, Insta or Gmail
